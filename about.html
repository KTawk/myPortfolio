Hi, I’m Kairly!


I’m currently based in Ottawa, where I’m pursuing a Bachelor’s in Software Engineering at the University of Ottawa. I’m passionate about designing inclusive digital and hardware solutions that empower people of all abilities. I’m committed to continuous learning and making a meaningful impact.

My latest project was prototyping a Bluetooth integrated mouse click button for a wheelchair user with limited hand dexterity. That device won top honours at uOttawa’s Engineering Design Day.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me carving fresh powder on my snowboard or unwinding at the piano. I love travelling to explore the beauty of the world and spreading positivity wherever I go. I also enjoy swimming and taking early morning walks when everything is still quiet.
